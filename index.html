About Me

I’m Aidan Miglio, also known as “prodbymigz,” and I’m a 19 year old producer from Long Island, New York. I am currently enrolled at Loyola University, New Orleans. In terms of production, hiphop is my speciality; more specifically underground hiphop. I listen to a lot of pluggnb, taking influence from artists like “KA$HDAMI,” and “Autumn!.” Besides production, I also have experience as a recording and mixing engineer in hiphop music. I have been producing my own, and helping produce others music for close to 2 years now. I have worked with rap artists from all over the United States, New York to California. Some of those artists include, “Gid Tha Illest,” “luh jag,” “JustJocin,” and “Kill Cam.”

Music

I specialize in underground hiphop music, specifically plugg and ambient production. I mostly work with people at my school, but sell beats to artists all around the country.
